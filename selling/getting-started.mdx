# Start Selling

AgentCraft is a marketplace where you can sell your agent configurations to other builders. If you've built configs that work well, package them up and earn from your expertise.

## Create your seller account

1. **Sign up** at [agntcraft.co/signup](https://agntcraft.co/signup)
2. **Connect Stripe** — go to Settings and click "Connect Stripe." This sets up your payout account through Stripe Connect Express. You'll need a bank account or debit card for receiving payouts.
3. **Complete onboarding** — Stripe walks you through identity verification. This usually takes under 5 minutes.

Once Stripe is connected, you can create paid listings immediately.

## Create your first listing

1. Go to [agntcraft.co/create-listing](https://agntcraft.co/create-listing)
2. Fill in the listing details:
   - **Title** — clear, descriptive name (e.g., "CEO Agent for Paperclip — Full Config Package")
   - **Description** — what the buyer gets, what framework it's for, what problem it solves
   - **Category** — Agents, Teams, Automations, Workflows, Guides, or Skills
   - **Framework** — OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex, Paperclip, Hermes, or Generic
   - **Price** — free or paid ($0.99 minimum, no maximum)
   - **Files** — upload your config files (SOUL.md, AGENTS.md, etc.)
3. Preview and publish

## Pricing tips

- **Free listings** build your reputation. Start with 1-2 free configs to get reviews and build trust.
- **$4.99 - $9.99** is the sweet spot for single-agent configs.
- **$14.99 - $29.99** works for multi-agent team templates or configs with detailed guides.
- **$49.99+** is reasonable for comprehensive automation packages with integration configs and setup guides.

## What sells well

- Configs for specific use cases (e.g., "Twitter Growth Agent," "Code Review Bot")
- Multi-agent team templates with coordination rules
- Framework-specific optimizations (e.g., "OpenClaw character.json + SOUL.md combo")
- Configs with detailed heartbeat routines and real-world tested workflows
