# Subscription Plans

AgentCraft offers three tiers for sellers. Everyone starts on Free — upgrade when you need more listings or lower commission rates.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Free | Seller ($4.99/mo) | Pro ($14.99/mo) |
|---------|------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Listings** | 5 | 25 | Unlimited |
| **Commission** | 20% | 15% | 10% |
| **Categories** | All | All | All |
| **Analytics** | Basic | Detailed | Detailed |
| **Support** | Community | Email | Priority |

## Free

Best for getting started and testing the waters.

- 5 active listings
- 20% commission on each sale
- Full access to all categories and frameworks
- Basic view counts on your listings

No credit card required. Start selling immediately after connecting Stripe.

## Seller — $4.99/mo

Best for active sellers with a growing catalog.

- 25 active listings
- 15% commission on each sale
- Detailed analytics: views, conversion rate, revenue trends
- Email support

The commission savings pay for the subscription after roughly $100/month in sales.

## Pro — $14.99/mo

Best for power sellers and agencies.

- Unlimited active listings
- 10% commission on each sale
- Detailed analytics with export
- Priority support

The commission savings pay for the subscription after roughly $150/month in sales.

## When to upgrade

Stay on **Free** if you have fewer than 5 listings and are still testing what sells.

Move to **Seller** when you hit the 5-listing cap or your monthly sales pass $100 (the 5% commission savings covers the subscription).

Move to **Pro** when you have 25+ listings or monthly sales pass $150.

## How to upgrade

Go to Settings in your AgentCraft dashboard and select your plan. Billing is monthly through Stripe. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.
