# How Commission Works

When you make a sale on AgentCraft, a commission is deducted before your payout. The rate depends on your subscription tier.

## Commission rates

| Plan | Commission |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 20% |
| Seller ($4.99/mo) | 15% |
| Pro ($14.99/mo) | 10% |

## Stripe processing fees

Stripe charges **2.9% + $0.30** on every transaction. This is deducted from the gross sale price before the commission split. The seller absorbs Stripe fees.

## Example math

A $9.99 listing sold by a **Seller** tier account:

| Step | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Sale price | $9.99 |
| Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30) | -$0.59 |
| Net after Stripe | $9.40 |
| AgentCraft commission (15%) | -$1.41 |
| **Your payout** | **$7.99** |

The same listing on **Free** (20% commission):

| Step | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Sale price | $9.99 |
| Stripe fee | -$0.59 |
| Net after Stripe | $9.40 |
| AgentCraft commission (20%) | -$1.88 |
| **Your payout** | **$7.52** |

And on **Pro** (10% commission):

| Step | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Sale price | $9.99 |
| Stripe fee | -$0.59 |
| Net after Stripe | $9.40 |
| AgentCraft commission (10%) | -$0.94 |
| **Your payout** | **$8.46** |

## Payout timing

Payouts are processed through Stripe Connect Express. After Stripe's standard holding period (typically 2-7 business days for new accounts, faster once established), funds are deposited directly to your connected bank account or debit card.

## Free listings

No commission is charged on free listings. Free listings are a great way to build your reputation and attract buyers to your paid configs.

## All sales are final

AgentCraft does not offer buyer refunds through the platform. All sales are final. Refunds are handled manually by admins on a case-by-case basis.
