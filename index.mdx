# Welcome to AgentCraft

AgentCraft is the platform for crafting AI agent configurations. Whether you're setting up your first agent or selling battle-tested configs, this is where you start.

## What AgentCraft does

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Wizard" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/quickstart">
    Answer questions about your agent. Get production-ready config files generated instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Marketplace" icon="store" href="https://agntcraft.co/browse">
    Browse and buy pre-made configs from experienced builders. Download and deploy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CLI" icon="terminal" href="/quickstart#cli">
    Generate configs from your terminal. Open source, zero dependencies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
